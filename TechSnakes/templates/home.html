About Tumor Trace

Tumor Trace is an AI-powered breast cancer detector designed to offer early and accurate detection of cancer. It utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms trained on extensive data to help users identify potential signs of cancer quickly and efficiently, ensuring peace of mind and timely medical intervention.
